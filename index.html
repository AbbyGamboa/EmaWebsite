ABOUT CONCHITA

Conchita was designed in 2023 as a homage to the popular mexican pan dulce of a similar name: the Concha. As a second generation Mexican-American child in Chicago, pan dulce was one way to maintain my Mexican culture. Besides conchas being a sugary delicious treat, the bread is a childhood memory I wanted to share with others of similar background but also give to those who appreciate Mexican culture.
